STEPS

Brownies

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat cake mix, oil, and eggs together. Pour into an 8x8 cake pan that has been lined with parchment paper.
  3. Bake for 20-23 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Remove from pan and allow the brownies to cool.

Cream Cheese Frosting

  1. Prepare frosting by beating cream cheese, butter, and vanilla together with a mixer until fluffy.
  2. Add half of powdered sugar and mix well.
  3. Add remaining powdered sugar and beat until uniform.
  4. When the brownies have cooled add the frosting, and top with sprinkles if desired.
